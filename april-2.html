April 2nd 2019: Using the built-in Mail app to manage email accounts such as Gmail and Outlook

Windows 10 has an in-built email app called Mail. It is possible to use it to manage several accounts. The advantage of this is your email accounts are centralised in one program. We will look at using Mail and how to manage (multiple) accounts such as Gmail and Outlook.

The resource links below will help you through the process of setting up Mail.
