Windows 10 Interest Group Resources

Introduction

The aim of this group is to help users of Windows 10 get the most out of their computer. Various features of Windows 10 will be explored (e.g. File Explorer), along with Microsoft apps such as Mail and Photos, plus free installable apps, such CCleaner and Irfanview. Web based apps such as Photopea will also be looked at. Below is information and resources on what is covered in each session.

March 5th 2019: Transferring photos from phones and cameras

Transferring photos from phones and cameras to a PC is a common practice. In this session we will look at how to do this using: 1) a USB cable and, 2) using Bluetooth wireless transfer.

Resource links:
  • How I download photos from my phone to my laptop
  • How to Share Files Over Bluetooth in Windows 10
  • How to import photos from iPhone to Windows 10
  • Complete Guide to Importing Your Photos in Windows 10

February 5th 2019: File and Folder Management

Managing files and folders in an efficient manner is one of the core skills necessary to be a competent computer user. This session will look at:

  • The tree-like structure that Windows uses to organise files
  • The naming of files and folders
  • How to organise files into folders and sub-folders
  • How to select, move, copy, delete, and rename files and folders

If time allows we will look at managing multiple windows.

Resource links:
  • Working with files
  • Tips for managing multiple windows

November 6th 2018: Gmail + Photos app

In this session we will revisit some of Gmail's features that we looked at in the last session. E.g. labels, filters, and using the Gmail basic HTML view. We will also have a look at how to use a selection of fun emojis/pictographs in the subject line and body of an email. Then we will start exploring the features of the Windows 10 Photos app. E.g. Importing photos and Collections, picture editing, slide and video shows.
