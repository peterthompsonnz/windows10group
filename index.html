Windows Interest Group Resources

Introduction

The aim of this group is to help users of the Windows operating systems to get the most out of their computer. Various features of Windows will be explored (e.g. File Explorer), along with Microsoft (Windows 10) apps such as Mail and Photos, plus free installable apps, such CCleaner and Irfanview. Web based apps such as Photopea will also be looked at.
