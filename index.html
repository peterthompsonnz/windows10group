Windows 10 Interest Group Resources

Introduction

The aim of this group is to help users of Windows 10 get the most out of their computer. Various features of Windows 10 will be explored (e.g. File Explorer), along with Microsoft apps such as Mail and Photos, plus free installable apps, such CCleaner and Irfanview. Web based apps such as Photopea will also be looked at.
