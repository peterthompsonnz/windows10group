May: Another look at the Mail app and exploring the privacy features of the Firefox web browser

Last session we looked at how to manage several email accounts using the Mail app. This session we will recap what we learned and then explore the privacy features of the Firefox web browser. We will also look at other interesting options Firefox gives, such as the ability to add privacy add-ons such as uBlock Origin.
