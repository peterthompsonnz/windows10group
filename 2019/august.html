Exploring the Spotify streaming music app

Streaming is a popular way of listening to music. Spotify is the most well known streaming music app. Users have access to a huge number of songs and music of all genres. The basic version of the app is free to use and versions are available for laptop/desktop, tablet and phone.

We started looking at Spotify last month. In this session we will revise what we learned and explore more features, such as:

  • Setting up an account
  • Account settings
  • Installing the app
  • Exploring the app's interface
  • Playing music
  • Saving music to Liked Songs
  • Albums and Artists
  • Creating a playlist
