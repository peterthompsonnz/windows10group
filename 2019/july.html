Exploring the Groove music app and another look at how to transfer files from a phone to a computer

The Groove music app is installed by default in Windows 10. Using it you can: play music files, rip CDs, arrange music files into albums and create playlists. We will explore these features.

We will also reacquaint ourselves with how to load files from a phone onto a computer.
